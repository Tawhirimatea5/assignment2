Sword Art Online Tierlist

This is a rating of each arc of Sword Art Online based on my personal opinion.

This is my personal tierlist for all released arcs of SAO. You can watch SAO at Netflix or on Anime streaming website Crunchyroll.
(An Arc known as Sword Art Online:Progressive is coming out later this year, it is considered a retelling of the first arc and would rate at the same level or above.)

Sword Art Online has technically 4 seasons(the 3rd season was split into two parts) and 1 movie with a second releasing in December with a fifth season in unannounced development. The chronological watch order is as follows:

  • Sword Art Online Season 1/Sword Art Online:Progressive the Movie, Aria of a Starless Night(retelling of Aincrad Arc in detail), consisting of the Aincrad Arc(ranked S tier) and Elfheim Arc(Ranked C tier)
  • Sword Art Online Season 2, consisting of the Phantom Bullet Arc(Ranked B tier), Mothers Rosario Arc(Ranked S tier), and the Excalibur Arc(Ranked C tier)
  • Sword Art Online Ordinal Scale the Movie(Ranked A tier)
  • Sword Art Online Season 3, Consisting of the Multiple Alicization Arcs(Ranked A tier)
  • Sword Art Online Season 4, The War of the Underworld Arc(Ranked S Tier)
